# GreenVerse: The Technical Whitepaper
**"Virtual Actions, Real World Impact"**

## 1. Project Overview
GreenVerse is a decentralized environmental oversight platform that bridges the gap between digital interaction and physical ecological restoration. By gamifying sustainable actions (recycling, tree planting, energy saving) and verifying them through a "Guardian Oversight" protocol, we create an immutable record of positive planetary impact.

## 2. Why GreenVerse?
Traditional environmental efforts often lack transparency and immediate gratification. GreenVerse solves this by:
- **Immutability**: Every approved action is signed onto a blockchain.
- **Incentivization**: Users earn liquid Eco-Tokens and unique NFT assets for their labor.
- **Scalability**: A crowdsourced "Guardian" network validates global proof-of-work.

---

## 3. The Neural Stack (Technology)

### **React & Vite** (Frontend)
- **Why**: High-performance, reactive UI that provides sub-second feedback for user interactions. Vite ensures extremely fast development cycles and optimized builds.
- **Aesthetic**: We use Tailwind CSS and Lucide for a "Neural/Eco-Brutalist" design language.

### **Node.js & Express** (Backend)
- **Why**: Asynchronous, non-blocking architecture perfect for handling multiple Web3 interactions and file uploads simultaneously.
- **Scaling**: Standardized REST API patterns allow for seamless integration with mobile apps and third-party eco-sensors.

### **PostgreSQL & Supabase** (Data Layer)
- **Why**: While blockchain stores the "Proof of Impact," standard data remains in a relational database for performance and complex querying (e.g., user profiles, history logs).
- **Synergy**: Supabase provides the robust PostgreSQL engine used for auditing and rapid search.

---

## 4. Web3 & MetaMask
### **What is MetaMask?**
MetaMask is a digital wallet and a bridge to the decentralized web (Web3). It stores your cryptographic keys and allows you to interact with blockchain smart contracts safely.

### **Why use it?**
1. **Identity**: Your wallet address serves as your unique, decentralized ID.
2. **Assets**: Your earned Eco-Tokens and NFT Trees are sent directly to your wallet; they are truly yours, not owned by GreenVerse.
3. **Security**: Every major action (like a swap or a mint) requires a cryptographic signature that only you can provide via MetaMask.

---

## 5. The Blockchain Engine
We utilize **Ethereum-compatible networks (EVM)**—specifically optimized for **Polygon**—to host our smart contracts.

### **How Rewards Work**
1. **Neural Submission**: A user uploads evidence of an eco-action.
2. **Guardian Review**: An administrator uses the **Guardian Console** to inspect the evidence.
3. **Smart Contract Trigger**:
   - **ECO Token (ERC-20)**: Issued as a liquid reward based on the impact score.
   - **NFT Tree (ERC-721)**: Minted as a unique digital asset representing the specific action (e.g., a "Virtual Oak" for a plantation).

### **Why use Blockchain?**
- **Immutability**: No one can delete your impact record.
- **Decentralization**: The rewards are distributed by code, not human whim.
- **Interoperability**: Your NFT Trees can eventually be traded or used in other metaverses.
